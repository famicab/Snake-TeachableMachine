Snake Game


Instructions

  1. Press "Start".
  2. Allow access to your device's camera.
  3. Make any movement to start the game.
  4. You can see an example here.

Controls

  1. Left: raise your left hand and show your palm.
  2. Right: raise your right hand and show your palm.
  3. Up: Over your head, make a pyramid with both hands.
  4. Down: Under your chin, make an inverse pyramid with both hands
